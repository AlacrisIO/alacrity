Prerequisites

To run Rock, Paper, Scissors you need to use a Web3-compatible browser and grant us permission to use Web3.

If your browser does not support Web3 natively, consider downloading the MetaMask extension.

How to play

Start a new game, or play an existing game.

WARNING

If you play first and the opponent can deny you access to the Internet via a DDoS attack, or otherwise cut your access to the Ethereum blockchain, they can steal your wager and your escrow. This game supposes that you will remain reliably connected to the blockchain for the duration of the game, especially as the first player of the game, who is on notice to reveal their hand within a deadline after the second player shows theirs.

When running this code on the Rinkeby test network, we use a timeout of 10 blocks (about 2.5 minutes). On the actual network, we use a timeout of 11520 blocks (about 2 days).
